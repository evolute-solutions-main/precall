This IS for you if

  • You're an established contractor already doing $50K/month or more
  • You have strong sales skills and a good business reputation

This is NOT for you if

04 / THE BIG PICTURE

Scaling A Contracting Company Is Simple

It comes down to two things: generating leads and booking appointments with them. That's literally the main driver of your business's growth — and we take care of 100% of it, so all you need to do is show up to the appointments we schedule for you.

Problem: Relying on word of mouth

Even if you have a HUGE month, next month could look completely different. Growth plateaus, and you're stuck working on smaller projects to fill gaps.

Solution: Consistent lead generation

Our system delivers a consistent flow of your ideal projects every month, allowing you to hire confidently and focus on the most profitable work.

Problem: Buying shared leads from vendors

Leads shop around for lowest prices, your info is shared with competitors, and many leads are suspicious or low quality.

Solution: Your own exclusive leads

We create ads under YOUR brand. When someone responds, they're asking for YOU specifically — not shopping around with multiple contractors.

Problem: Wasting time chasing leads

You're a business owner, not a cold caller. Most contractors are completely unaware of how many touchpoints are needed to convert a lead.

Solution: Automated follow-up + qualification

Our AI and human team handle every call, text, and email — sometimes following up for 3+ weeks until a lead is ready to schedule.

Problem: Meeting cold prospects

Without prep, leads forget appointments, you arrive as a stranger, and you're stuck doing 100% of the selling on the spot.

Solution: Pre-appointment indoctrination

We send your best work, testimonials, and differentiators BEFORE the meeting. When you arrive, they already trust you and understand your value.
